Futures! Museum Exhibit: Park Planner

Experience Designer and Content Developer

"Park Planner" was an interactive installation featured in the Smithsonian's "FUTURES" exhibition at the Arts and Industries Building. This engaging experience allowed visitors to design their ideal park by selecting various futuristic elements such as playgrounds, gardens, and sports facilities. As participants made their choices, the installation provided feedback on the environmental and social impacts of their designs, fostering a deeper understanding of sustainable urban planning. By blending agency with education, "Park Planner" inspired visitors to envision and contribute to the development of greener, more inclusive public spaces.
