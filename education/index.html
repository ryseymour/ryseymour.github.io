Social Prescence in Online Courses

Online courses can either succeed or fail based on whether there are opportunities for students to feel that they are part of the learning community. However, just because students and teachers cannot meet doesn't mean that the learning environment needs to suffer. These resources can help faculty develop strategies to improve the social presecene of participants in an online course.

  • Edgar Garcia-O'Neill-Social Presence In Online Learning: 7 Things Instructional Designers Can Do To Improve It
