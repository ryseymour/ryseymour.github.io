Social Presence in Online Courses

Online courses can either succeed or fail based on whether there are opportunities for students to feel that they are part of the learning community. However, just because students and teachers cannot meet doesn't mean that the learning environment needs to suffer. These resources can help faculty develop strategies to improve the social presence of participants in an online course.

This section looks at how technology can be used to enhance higher level learning experiences, through the implementation of Project Based lessons.

  • Project Based Online Learning: Meeting the Challenge

    • Project Based Learning in an online environment can be challenging when students cannot meet in person. This post describes an attempt by researchers to overcome these hindrances so that no matter the distance students can participate in a PBL model.
