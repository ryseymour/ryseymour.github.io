Minecraft Education: Mobile

Technical Product Manager

As the Technical Program Manager for the mobile expansion of Minecraft Education, I spearheaded the initiative to make this impactful educational platform available on iOS and Android, broadening its reach and accessibility for millions of educators and students worldwide. Mini Golf Motion

Lead Developer and Designer

Mini Golf Motion is an educational physics game that teaches students about force, angles, and motion. Designed as an interactive and enjoyable game, it allows students to apply physics concepts in a mini-golf setting, reinforcing learning through gameplay. This project provides an engaging platform for students to experiment with variables, observe outcomes, and gain a practical understanding of physics principles.

Pick Your Plate

Game Designer

Pick Your Plate is an interactive educational game developed to teach students about cultural diversity through food. Players explore dietary choices from different countries, learning about balanced diets, nutrition, and cultural practices around the world. This game fosters global awareness and encourages healthy eating habits by introducing players to a variety of cuisines.

Tami's Tower

Game Designer

Tami's Tower is a fun physics-based puzzle game that challenges players to build a stable tower to help Tami the monkey reach her bananas. Through trial and error, players learn about balance, gravity, and stability, making this game an excellent tool for teaching foundational physics concepts in a playful and interactive way.
