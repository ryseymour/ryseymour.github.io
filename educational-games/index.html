Tami's Tower

Game Designer

"Tami's Tower" is an interactive game aimed at teaching STEM concepts. Players help Tami, the red panda, build a tower to reach food by solving engineering challenges. The game promotes spatial reasoning, problem-solving skills, and a foundational understanding of physics.
